# Safe Patient & Equipment Transfer Simulation

## Overview
This Python project simulates safe patient and equipment transfers in a hospital setting. It ensures hospital safety rules are followed when moving patients and medical devices between wards.

## Objectives
- Simulate hospital ward transfers of patients and equipment
- Enforce safety rules to prevent patient risks
- Demonstrate nurse workflow and decision-making in patient transport

## Features
- Moves patients and equipment safely
- Validates rules such as:
  - Certain patients cannot be left alone
  - Equipment requiring supervision cannot be left unattended
- Step-by-step simulation with feedback

## Technologies
- Python 3

## Healthcare Relevance
Patient and equipment transfer is a critical process in hospitals. This simulation demonstrates practical applications of safety rules and workflow management to prevent accidents and improve patient care quality.

## Future Improvements
- Integrate with a hospital EMR to log transfers
- Add multiple wards and staff assignments
- Include real-time alerts for unsafe transfer attempts
- Visualize transfers via GUI or animation
